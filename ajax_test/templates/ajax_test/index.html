FRnet-DTI: Deepnet for Feature Manipulation and Drug-Target Interaction Prediction

Abstract
In this paper, we present FRnet-DTI, an auto encoder and a convolutional classifier for feature manipulation and drug target interaction prediction.Two convolutional neural methods are aimed where one model is used for feature manipulation and the other one for classification. Using the 1st method, FRnet-1, we generate 4096 features from 1476 of each datasets and use the second method, FRnet-2, to identify interaction probability employing those features. We have tested our method on four gold standard datasets, exhaustively used by other researchers, where our method shows significant improvement over the state-of-the-art method on three of the 4 drug-target interaction gold standard datasets on both area under curve for Receiver Operating Characteristic(auROC) and area under Precision Recall curve(auPR) metric. We also introduce 20 new Drug-Target pair based on high prediction score
